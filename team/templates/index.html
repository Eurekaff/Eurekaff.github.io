With the rapid development of The Times, the consumption and market of electronic products are constantly expanding, but the old electronic equipment is constantly abandoned, resulting in the disposal and recycling of waste electronic products.

However, China's recycling of electronic waste in the recycling rate and ways are unsatisfactory. According to a 2019 data. The global average recycling rate of e-waste is 17.4%, while China's recycling rate is only about 10%.

We hope that through this environmentally friendly treatment method, the purpose of recycling precious metals in e-waste can be achieved, while reducing the direct or potential damage to the environment as much as possible, so that the treatment and reuse of e-waste is no longer difficult, and can truly contribute to the protection of resources and the environment efficiently.
